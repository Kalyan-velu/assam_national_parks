6. Raimona National Park

See More...

It lies in Kokrajhar district of the Bodoland Territorial Region in the lower Assam. It was declared as a National Park on 5 June 2021. The boundary of Raimona National Park is marked by the Sankosh River on the west, along inter-state boundary of West-Bengal and Assam from Indo-Bhutan border up to fire line ride - 6 Southwards (BP30).
Raimona National Park is rich in Biodiversity. It has four non-human primates, stow birds, assamese macaque Rhesus monkey & capped longur.
