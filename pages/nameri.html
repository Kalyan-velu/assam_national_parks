4. Nameri National Park

See More...

The Nameri National Park of 200 sq. kms. area is located in the Sonitpur district, bordering Arunachal Pradesh and it is also the core area of Nameri Tiger Reserve (344 Sq. Km.). One can enjoy rafting in the Jia Bhoroli river in the south-western boundary. It has a designation tiger reserve under project Tiger. Nameri lies on the interstate border between Assam and Arunachal Pradesh. The Jia-Bharali river flow through the park along with its tributaris:

  • The Nameri River
  • Upper Dikorai River
  • Bor Dikorai River
Nameri National Park is home to various globally theatened birds and mammals. The most secure popuation of the endangered species white winged duck is found here on the North Bank of the Brahmaputra river. It is famous for its populatiion of Asian elephants and tigers.
